About Me

Welcome to my world!

Hey there! I'm Muhammad Omar, a passionate coder and Full Stack Developer based in London. I find immense joy in bringing websites to life through captivating animations and stunning designs. The ability to create intuitive user experiences is something I constantly strive for in my work.

Family is everything to me. I'm a proud father of two amazing children who are my purpose in life. They inspire me to push my boundaries and excel in everything I do. I'm incredibly fortunate to have a supportive wife who encourages me to chase my dreams.

I firmly believe in the power of continuous learning. There's no task that I consider unachievable when I set my mind to it. My unwavering determination and curiosity drive me to explore new technologies, expand my skill set, and stay ahead in the ever-evolving world of development.

In my free time, you'll find me indulging in my passions. I love playing and watching football, going on memorable holidays with my family and friends, immersing myself in the world of video games, expanding my knowledge through reading, taking leisurely walks with my American Akita named Hachi, and of course, both cooking and savoring delicious meals.

Looking into the future, my aspirations extend beyond coding. When I eventually retire, I hope to have developed software or websites that simplify people's lives and streamline their daily tasks. My goal is to make a positive impact, particularly for those who juggle multiple responsibilities and struggle to find time for themselves and their dreams.
